Comp 371 assignment 2

This opengl project represents a 3d model of a car with texturing, lighting and shadow

Controls:

-Home key: Recents camera to original position when window is created

-Arrow keys: Rotate camera around the world

-L key: Transforms car parts into visible vertices

-P key: Tranforms all car parts into visible individual vertex points

-T key: transforms all car parts by filling their vertices

-U key: Upscale the car

-J key: downscale the car

-R key: resets car parts back to their original position

-WASDQR: move car in various directions

-X: turn off textures for car

-N: turn on textures for car
